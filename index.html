BUSINESS

Auction

The Chittagong Tea Auction is the main auction house of the tea industry in Bangladesh . The auctions are held every Tuesday .Tea traders from different places in Bangladesh who are registered with the Bangladesh Tea Board & members of Tea Traders Association of Bangladesh attend. Tea produced by our companies also through the auction. The CLEVEDON Tea estate a tea garden of HRC group emerged the best garden of the country during last 13 years for highest auction price.

Domestic Market

Tea is not just a beverage , it’s a life style. Slow down and relax , brew a cup of tea that is not only delicious to taste buds but also great for body. HRC is a popular brand name in Tea sector. Currently 13 different blends of Tea in 36 different pack size (SKU) marketed to cater all segments & taste buds of consumers. HRC currently enjoys approx. around 20% of the blended packet tea market share.

Export

Over the year HRC has internationally established it self as a reliable and quality exporter of Bangladesh Black Tea. The Government of Bangladesh has granted “CIP” status to HRC from 1991/1992for its export achievements. Till date HRC has received eleven(11) National export trophies ( 9 Gold + 2 Silver). Exports are primarily to Russia , Poland , Afganistan , Kazakstan , Uzbekistan , PRC , UAE , Pakistan , Cyprus , and Sudan. Since inception HRC has on an average exported annually 20.42% of country`s total export.
